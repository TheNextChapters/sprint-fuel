⚡ KEY TRAINING PRINCIPLES

Never skip the 30-minute post-practice recovery window — this is where gains are made or lost
Morning activation is 20 min max — mobility, core, CNS priming only before school
Tue & Thu gym sessions start no later than 6PM for full recovery before bed
On track days: arrive 15 min early for dynamic warm-up before coach-led session
Saturday = active recovery ONLY. Sunday = full mental and physical rest, no exceptions
Always land with soft knees on plyos. If legs feel dead, cut plyo volume in half

📈 4-WEEK PERFORMANCE TIMELINE

WEEK 1
Volume & Foundation
Learn movements, build base. Soreness peaks Day 3–4 then drops.
WEEK 2
Push PRs
Heavy barbell work. Block starts feel more explosive. CNS adapting.
WEEK 3
Speed + Strength
Flying 30s feel noticeably faster. PRs start happening this week.
WEEK 4
Taper & Race
Reduce gym 30%, maintain sprint intensity. Body primed for low 10s.

✅ DAILY RECOVERY CHECKLIST

⚡ NUTRITION NON-NEGOTIABLES

💪
Protein: 0.8–1g per lb of bodyweight per day (160 lb = 160g). Non-negotiable.
⚡
Carbs are your fuel: Brown rice, sweet potato, oats, banana, pasta are all good. Never skip carbs before practice.
💧
Hydration: 100oz water minimum daily. Add electrolytes (Liquid IV, LMNT, Nuun) on training days.
🚫
No junk food within 3 hours of practice — you will feel slow and heavy on the track.
🥤
Post-practice window (30 min): Protein shake (25–30g) + fast carb. This is critical — don't miss it.
🌙
9PM snack: Casein protein or Greek yogurt with PB — slow-digesting protein feeds muscles overnight.
✝️
Fridays through Easter: ZERO MEAT. Fish, eggs, dairy, veggies only. No exceptions.
🚫
Never eat: Italian Hoagie, Philly Cheesesteak, Meatball Sub, Buffalo Chicken specials with pork.

🏋️ WEEKLY RECOVERY MILESTONES

By end of Week 1: Body adapted to volume. Soreness will peak Day 3–4 then decrease.
By end of Week 2: Block starts and reaction time feel more explosive. CNS adapting.
By end of Week 3: Top-end speed sessions feel noticeably faster. PRs start happening.
Week 4 (Taper): Reduce gym volume 30%, maintain sprint intensity. Body primed to run fast.
