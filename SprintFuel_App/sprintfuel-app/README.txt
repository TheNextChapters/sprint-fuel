HOW TO LAUNCH (picks up where your browser left off — data saves locally)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

▶ WINDOWS:
   Double-click: "Launch Sprint Fuel (Windows).bat"
   → Opens as a standalone app window (no browser UI, no address bar)
   → If Chrome or Edge is installed it launches in full App Mode
   → Your data saves in the "chrome-data" folder next to this file

▶ MAC:
   Double-click: "Launch Sprint Fuel (Mac).command"
   → If it says "cannot be opened", right-click → Open → Open
   → Opens in Chrome App Mode (no browser UI)

▶ ALTERNATIVE (any computer):
   Just double-click "SprintFuel_Tracker.html" to open in your browser
   Pin the tab by right-clicking it → "Pin Tab"

YOUR DATA
---------
All data is saved in your browser's local storage.
It stays there between sessions as long as you don't clear browser data.
If you use the launcher, data is saved in the "chrome-data" folder.
